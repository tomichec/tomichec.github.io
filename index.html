Overview

I am currently a Postdoctoral Research Associate at the University of Exeter, UK working under program Analysis and Design for Accelerated Production of Tailored Composites (ADAPT) for airospace engineering.

I have finished a PhD degree in mathematical biology at the University of Exeter, UK focused on mathematical models of electric current in the heart (cardiac electrophysiology). I have recieved my master in biomedical engineering from the Polytechnic University of Valencia, Spain and a degree in electrical engineering from VSB-TU Ostrava, Czech republic.

Please visit to find out more about my past research, skills and publications.

Please, download my CV and get in touch if there is anything I could do for you.
